Real-Time Learning Experience with Mission 42

A mobile, smart city solution that supports real world community learning.

  • Real-World Learning

    Lets users learn about buildings, objects, art work, and more in their local neighborhoods- so they start to see how learning applies in the real world!

  • Gamification

    Presents learning activities as fun learning missions!

  • Teamwork and Community Engagement

    Lets students engage classmates and community members for support along the way! And share their completed missions with the community.

How It Works !

Itâ€™s simple! Teachers and parents can pull from learning templates or create custom learning content for students. Students see the learning content as missions and are given a set amount of time to complete each mission. Each learning mission is tagged to a nearby learning artifact, whether a building, object, or something in nature.

Along the way, students have fun, learn the importance of teamwork, and start to see how learning applies in the real world.
